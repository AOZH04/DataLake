    Polygons
    Our platform allows you to get ready-made segmentation datasets tailored for your task with just one click. No need to set up data labeling environments; just visit the site, select the objects that need to be labeled, and train your AI technology in one day.
    Texts
    Our platform allows you to compile image datasets with textual descriptions attached to them. In the search bar, you can specify a list of questions that should be addressed in the textual description of each item in the acquired data. The resulting dataset can be used to train your own Generative AI technology.
